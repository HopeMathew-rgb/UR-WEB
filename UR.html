WITH UR AT THE FOREFRONT

The University of Rwanda (UR) is the largest higher education institution in Rwanda, committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement. Established in 2013 through the merger of several public institutions, UR offers a wide range of academic programs across various disciplines, fostering innovation and contributing to the socio-economic development of Rwanda and the region.
